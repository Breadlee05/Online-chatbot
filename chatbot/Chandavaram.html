Chandavaram Buddhist site

Description

The Chandavaram Buddhist site, located in the Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh, India, is an ancient archaeological site dating back to around the 2nd century BCE to the 2nd century CE. It features a stupa, rock-cut caves, and monastic cells, which provide insights into early Buddhist practices and architecture in southern India. The site is notable for its historical significance and contributions to our understanding of early Buddhism in the region.
