Bhagwan Mahavir Government Museum

Description

The Bhagwan Mahavir Government Museum, located in the city of Patna, Bihar, India, is a prominent museum showcasing a diverse collection of artifacts. Named after the Jain Tirthankara Bhagwan Mahavir, the museum features exhibits related to art, archaeology, and history. Highlights include ancient sculptures, coins, manuscripts, and artifacts from various periods of Indian history. It serves as a valuable resource for understanding the rich cultural and historical heritage of the region.
