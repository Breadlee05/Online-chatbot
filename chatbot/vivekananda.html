Vivekananda rock memorial

Description

The Vivekananda Rock Memorial is a striking monument located on a rocky island off the coast of Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu. Dedicated to Swami Vivekananda, it commemorates his meditation here in 1892. The memorial features a blend of traditional Indian architectural styles and includes a statue of Vivekananda, along with meditation and assembly halls. Accessible by ferry, it offers stunning views of the surrounding seas and serves as a site of spiritual reflection and inspiration.
